<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	<h4>PROVIDE WORK PERMIT FOR FOREIGNER LABORERS WORKING IN VIETNAM</h4></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	I. Purpose</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	To manage the recruitment of foreign laborers by enterprises and organizations in Vietnam</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	&nbsp;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	II. Who should apply?</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	All enterprises and organizations recruiting foreign laborers to work in Vietnam, except:</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	- Foreign laborers entering Vietnam to work for a period of less than 03 (three) months; and those entering Vietnam to resolve and emergency situation such as a breakdown or a technically or technologically complex situation which suddenly arises and which affects, or has the risk of affecting production or business and which Vietnamese experts and foreign experts in Vietnam are not able to resolve</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	- Foreigners who are members of the Board of Management or member&rsquo;s council (in the case of a limited liability company) established under Vietnamese laws with legal status</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	- Foreigners who are head of representative offices or branches in Vietnam</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	- Foreign lawyers to whom Ministry of Justice has issued a certificate to practice in accordance with laws</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	&nbsp;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	III. Where to apply?</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	Department of Labor&nbsp;War Invalid and Social Affair&nbsp;in the locality where enterprises locate their headquarters</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	&nbsp;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	IV. Validity of the License</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	Duration:</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	Same duration as the duration of the labor contract or as set out in the decision of the foreign party on appointment, but shall not exceed 36 months.</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	V. Eligibility of Applicants</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	1. Being 18 years of age or older</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	2. Being physically fit to the work requirements</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	3. Must have highly technical skills or highly professional qualifications (including: engineers and persons of a standards similar to or higher than engineers; traditional trade artisans) and be very experienced and be senior professionally in the direction of production or business operation or in management work which Vietnamese employees are not yet able to perform.</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	4. Have no previous conviction or criminal record and must not currently be subject to criminal prosecution or any criminal sentence in accordance with Vietnamese and foreign laws</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	5. Foreign employees engaged in private medical or pharmaceutical practices or directly carrying out disease examination and treatment in Vietnam must satisfy all conditions stipulated by the Vietnamese laws in relation to private medical or pharmaceutical practice.</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	&nbsp;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	VI. Application conditions and documents</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	Application dossiers, which should be prepared in 2 copies, shall consist of the following:</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	1. Application for issuance of work permit of the labor users;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	2. Application for working in Vietnam;</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	3. Form of criminal record issued by the competent authority from the foreign countries they lived. In case where the foreigners have resided in Vietnam for over six months, they should also submit the Form of criminal record issued by the Department of Justice in the locality where they are living.</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	4. CV of the foreign laborer&rsquo;s resume, made according to a form set by the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, including an attached photograph of the foreigner.</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	5. The process</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 1.6em; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; color: rgb(68, 68, 68);">	1.
